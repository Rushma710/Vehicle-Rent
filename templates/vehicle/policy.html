Vehicle Rental Policy And Privacy

1. General Information

By renting a vehicle with us, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. Please ensure you understand all guidelines, fees, and conditions before proceeding with your reservation.

2. Eligibility

The renter must be at least 21 years old with a valid driver's license. International drivers must have an International Driving Permit.

3. Rental Period

The rental period begins when you pick up the vehicle and ends when you return it. Additional charges may apply for any late returns.

4. Fuel Policy

The vehicle should be returned with the same fuel level as when rented. If not, a refueling fee will be applied to your bill.

5. Insurance Coverage

Basic insurance is included, but we highly recommend additional coverage for comprehensive protection. Damage to the vehicle will be charged if caused by the renter.

6. Security Deposit

A refundable security deposit will be charged at the time of booking. The deposit will be returned to you if the vehicle is returned in the same condition as when it was rented.

7. Prohibited Uses

The rental vehicle cannot be used for illegal activities, racing, or off-road driving. Any violations will result in immediate termination of the rental agreement.

8. Vehicle Theft

If the vehicle is stolen, you are required to report it immediately to the police and our rental office. Failure to report may result in full liability for the vehicle's replacement cost.

9. Cancellations and Refunds

To cancel your reservation, you must provide 48 hours' notice for a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours are subject to a cancellation fee.
